Info:

You can choose between several game types...
Play against another player choosing the PvP game type or challenge yourself against a CPU bot with 3 main difficulties.

Then to play (when you open a game session) you have just to select the piece to move and give it a direction to go selecting one of the marked moves it can do.

If you are stuck in a game, you can call a HINT to show all of your movable pieces by clicking the bottom left corner button
